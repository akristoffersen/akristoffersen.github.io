Alexander Kristoffersen

Hi! My name is Alex, I'm an engineer living in SF interested in exciting applications of CV and ML.

I recently started at Midjourney, where I am writing software within their hardware team.

From 2023-2025, I was an Autonomy Engineer, Computer Vision at Skydio primarily working within photogrammetry and 3D vision.

I graduated with a BS in EECS at UC Berkeley in 2022, and completed a Masters in EECS in 2023 specializing in computer vision and graphics.

Here are a couple of projects that I am proud to be a part of:

  • Nerfstudio - A collaboration-friendly studio for NeRFs (Paper)
  • Skydio Onboard Modeling (On-device photogrammetry product I helped ship in late 2023)
  • LoopNeRF - Temporal Compression for Generating 3D Video Textures (Master's Final Project)
  • Space Technologies at Cal (STAC)
  • Stegasaurus - Private Messaging Tool
  • RISELab - Undergraduate Researcher
  • And more to come!

I like computer vision, graphics, beekeeping, woodworking, slide rules, krautrock, funk music, satellites, signal processing, oranges, robotics, sculpting, traveling, generative modeling, folk and self-taught art, vintage clothing, and picking up new interests. I'm not so great at web design.

I have recently become interested in the intersection of machine learning and social choice, and presented a paper (co-authors Wesley Holliday and Eric Pacuit) at SCaLa-24 Workshop at AAMAS 2024 on the topic. It was also accepted as an oral presentation at AAAI 2025!
