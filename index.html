I recently joined the Autonomy Team at Skydio as a computer vision engineer, primarily working within photogrammetry and 3D vision.

  • LoopNeRF - Temporal Compression for Generating 3D Video Textures (Master's Final Project)
  • Space Technologies at Cal (STAC)
  • Stegasaurus - Private Messaging Tool
  • RISELab - Undergraduate Researcher
  • And more to come!
